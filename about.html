About Us

ATG Khidmat was established in 2009 with the vision of manufacturing all kind of eco-friendly Bags and promotional item.
We started with bags. And over time we started making promotional items as well. and now We are a proud and leading supplier of printed promotional products and corporate gifts with a vast range of quality items that can be printed or decorated to assist you in your next promotion or advertising program.
Promotional products are probably the most practical way to get your brand or message across at conferences and trade shows or events. So we make products keeping this in mind.
And gradually we have increased our product. Like we make Jute bags, Canvas bags, Cotton bags, Non-Woven Bags, Laptop Bags, Tote bags, Drawstring Bags, Travel Bags, Cash & Pouch Bags, Leather Bags, sythetic bags, paper bags and Trolley bags in bags. Apart from promotional items, we have started making umbrella canopies, caps, T-shirts. All of these have the same purpose that we make you get all the items under one roof.
